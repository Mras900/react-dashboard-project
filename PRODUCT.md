# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **Internal stakeholders**: Executives, operations managers, and supervisors who need daily visibility into business metrics (billing, complaints, visits).
- **External stakeholders**: Clients and regional managers who monitor performance across communes and geographic areas.
- **Context**: Office, mobile, laptop, large-screen displays (TV-sized dashboards for monitoring centers).

## Product Purpose

A real-time operational dashboard that transforms raw billing, complaint, and visit data into actionable insights by geographic area (communes). It enables:

- Quick visual assessment of daily metrics and health indicators
- Deep analysis for root-cause investigation and trend spotting
- Geographic intelligence (map-based filtering and analysis)
- Alert detection for high-priority issues
- Multi-level reporting (KPI aggregation, time-series, regional breakdowns)

Success = users can answer critical business questions in seconds ("What's blocking us today?", "Which communes are underperforming?", "Are we on target?") without manual spreadsheet work.

## Brand Personality

**Efficient · Clear · Precise · Agile**

This is a tool for operators who value their time. Every element should justify its presence. No decoration. No bloat. If it's there, it's because someone needs it to make a decision.

## Anti-references

- Generic dashboards with irrelevant information and no business value
- Decorative dashboards that prioritize "looks modern" over clarity
- Cluttered, high-noise interfaces that confuse rather than guide
- Overuse of colors, animations, or visual effects that distract from data

## Design Principles

1. **Data-first, no decoration**: Information is the protagonist. Visual style supports readability and hierarchy; it never competes with data.
2. **Efficient density**: Pack information intelligently. Responsive, scannable layouts that scale from mobile to 65" monitors without losing clarity.
3. **Precise over pretty**: Correct labeling, accurate numbers, clear provenance. Trust is built on precision, not polish.
4. **Responsive by nature**: Layout, typography, density adapt intelligently to context (mobile reconnaissance, laptop deep-dive, team room monitoring).
5. **Action-ready**: The interface guides users to next decisions. Alerts, trends, and comparisons are visible; irrelevant metrics are hidden.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** as baseline standard
- **Color blindness consideration**: Do not rely on color alone for distinction; use patterns, icons, and labels.
- **Reduced motion support**: Essential animations are optional; interface is fully functional without them.
- **Keyboard navigation and screen reader support**: All interactive elements are accessible.
- **Responsive text sizing**: Support zoom up to 200% without loss of functionality.
- **High contrast mode**: Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text).
